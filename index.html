Welcome To Battleship!

OBJECTIVE

Be the first to sink all of your opponent's ships!

GETTING STARTED

You have three ships (battleship - 4 spaces, submarine - 3 spaces, destroyer - 2 spaces) that you can place horizontally or vertically on any free board spaces. To place your ships, follow the below steps:

  1. Click on the picture of the ship you want to place on the board.
  2. Once you select a ship, use the arrow buttons to choose the direction you want the ship to face.
  3. Once you select a ship and direction, click the starting space for the ship on the player board.
  4. Repeat for each of your ships. Once you are ready to start the game, press the 'Start Game' button.

GAMEPLAY

You and the computer take alternating shots, each calling out one shot per turn. The player moves first.

  1. To make your shot, click one free space on the computer's board.
  2. Once you take a shot, the space will turn red if it's a hit, and gray if it's a miss.
  3. After you take your turn, the computer will take a shot. Whoever sinks all of the other player's ships first wins!
